Our Story

Mjekiqi-Service was founded with a clear vision: to deliver the highest quality in drywall construction and building management. What started as a small company has grown into a trusted partner for renovation and restoration projects throughout the region.

Our journey began with the conviction that every construction project is more than just a task – it's the realization of our customers' dreams and needs. This philosophy drives us every day and is reflected in every project we undertake.

Today, we're proud to work with an experienced team, cutting-edge techniques, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Our success is built on trust, transparency, and the continuous development of our skills.
